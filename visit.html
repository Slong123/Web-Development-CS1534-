Visit Kempton Park

Things to Do

Emperors Palace Casino

Located near O. R. Tambo International Airport, Emperors Palace is a large entertainment complex offering casinos, restaurants, theatres, and hotels, making it one of Kempton Park’s main attractions for visitors.

Festival Mall

Festival Mall is a popular shopping centre in Kempton Park featuring a wide range of retail stores, restaurants, and entertainment options, making it a convenient place for visitors to shop and relax.

Quick Facts
  • O. R. Tambo International Airport located in Kempton Park
  • About 20 km from Rietvlei Nature Reserve
  • Close to Johannesburg and Pretoria
  • Major transport hub in Gauteng
