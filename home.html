Kempton Park

South Africa, Gauteng

Information

Kempton Park lies in the heart of South Africa and is home to the country's largest international airport, O. R. Tambo International Airport. The city is located near the major urban centres of Johannesburg and Pretoria, making it an excellent starting point for tourism across South Africa. From Kempton Park, visitors can travel to destinations such as Kruger National Park in Limpopo Province or nearby nature reserves like Rietvlei Nature Reserve, located about 20 km away. Kempton Park and Gauteng lie on an elevated plateau with an average elevation of around 1,500 metres above sea level. This high elevation helps create a milder climate compared to many other regions of South Africa.
